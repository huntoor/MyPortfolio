About me

Hello! I’m Omar Ismail, a dynamic software engineer with a solid educational background from Ain Shams University. My tech career began in 2020, initially focusing on web development. My curiosity and passion for learning quickly led me to explore the realm of cyber security. This experience was transformative, enriching my ability to write secure, robust code and deepening my proficiency with Linux.

In 2021, my journey circled back to web development where I embraced new challenges in Node.js and React. During this period, I not only honed my frontend skills but also gained substantial knowledge in backend development and database management. This holistic view of web technologies has been instrumental in shaping my approach to software solutions.

My career took another exciting turn in 2023 when I entered the world of game development. This shift wasn’t just a change in focus—it was a discovery of my true passion. Designing and developing games has been a thrilling experience that combines my technical skills with creativity and storytelling. It’s a field where I see myself not just participating but innovating.

Here are some technologies I’ve learned along the way:

  • Game Development (Godot, Unity Engine, C#, GDScript)
  • Web Development (HTML/CSS, Javascript, React)
  • Programming Languages (Python, java, C/C++)
  • Databases (MySql, MariaDB, Firebase)
  • Mobile App Development (Flutter)
  • Version Control (Git)
